Travel to Seychelles, Fregate Island

About the Island

The private island of Fregate is an ultimate honeymooner's delight.
It is one of the world's most exclusive resorts with seven pristine beaches to choose from. Simply close your eyes and think idyllic.

What To Do:

  • Enjoy deep sea fishing.
  • Do yoga in the great outdoors.

Where To Go:

  • Go to nature walks.
  • Go mountain biking.
  • Get a scuba diving certificate.

What To Eat:

  1. Fresh grilled fish.
  2. Coconut Curry.
  3. Breadfruit.
  4. Sausage Rougay.
